Our Mission

Cakelicious first started as a small bakery to provide for the lesser in hard times to offer delicious and splendid baking. It was found by Conner Backa to spread Ottawa with traditional European baking goods with the local communities. It has since evolved to include many different fusions of barkery techniques and employed many different kind of new bakers to increase Cakelicious' palate. Each cake is uniquely hand-made from scratch by our specialists with love. Cakelicious is committed to use ingredients and packagings that come from natural and sustainable sources.
